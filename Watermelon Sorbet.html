Watermelon Sorbet Recipe

Ingredients

  • 8 cups cubed seeded watermelon
  • 2 medium limes, zested and juiced, divided
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 pinch chili-lime seasoning (such as Tajin)

Preparation Details

Prep time: 15 minutes

Additional time: 5 hours

Total time: 5 hours 15 minutes

Servings: 6

Steps

  1. Place watermelon cubes in a single layer on a sheet pan. Freeze at least 5 hours or overnight.
  2. Zest limes; set aside for garnish. Juice limes until you have 2 tablespoons juice.
  3. Place 1/2 of watermelon cubes in food processor with 1 tablespoon honey and 1 tablespoon lime juice. Blend until smooth and creamy and transfer to a large bowl. Repeat with remaining watermelon, honey, and lime juice.
  4. Stir the 2 batches together and serve immediately, garnished with lime zest and TajinÂ®. Or, place in a freezer-safe container and freeze for a firmer texture.
